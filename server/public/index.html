Project Stat's Mission

Our mission is to provide a free, online statistics course for underprivileged individuals in AEOP as a simple resource to assist in the development of their STEM skills. This online course will have an audience focus of grade 8 and higher, but other individuals are welcome to utilize the resource to strengthen their statistical instruction for competitive STEM fields.

Online Courses

Learn about statistics in our free online course. The topics covered in this course apply to all STEM fields.

Monitoring Tool

Teachers can register an entire class to take part in Project Stat's free online course.

Statistical Calculators

All users will have access to external statistical Calculators to aid in completing course content.
